Nine Men's Morris

Topic 1: Basic Rules

Placement Phase

Each player places one piece per turn on an empty point. Forming a mill allows you to remove an opponent's piece.

Movement Phase

Once all pieces are placed, players move their pieces to adjacent points. Forming a mill still lets you remove an opponent's piece.

Flying Phase

When a player has only three pieces left, they can "fly", moving a piece to any empty point on the board.

Topic 2: Advanced Rules

Special Moves

In certain cases, a player may move pieces in specific ways to gain an advantage.

Winning Strategies

Understanding how to control the board can help you win the game by blocking or outmaneuvering your opponent.
